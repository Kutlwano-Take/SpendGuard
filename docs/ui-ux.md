# UI/UX Guidelines

SpendGuard follows a luxury-minimalist design with high contrast, gold accents, and smooth micro-interactions.

## Principles
- Dark mode first, premium gradients, and subtle highlights.
- Minimal layouts with high information density but low cognitive load.
- Motion that feels elegant (no jarring transitions).

## Components
- **Dashboard**: Budget rings, AI tip carousel, glanceable stats.
- **Quick Add**: Modal or sheet with OCR option and category suggestions.
- **Insights**: Cards with trends and actionable tips.
- **Settings**: Simple toggles for alerts, privacy, and themes.

## Accessibility
- WCAG AA contrast targets.
- Keyboard navigation and screen reader support.
- Optional color-blind friendly palettes.
